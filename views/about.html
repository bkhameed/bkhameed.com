The Roots

Born in Anaheim, California - I attended community college and took my first computer science classes at Fullerton College, and found my passion for coding and building through Scott Edwards, my first and best CS professor. Later, I worked at Ultra Mobile as a software engineer intern, where I had my first taste of enterprise-level software development while studying at University of California, Irvine as a transfer. That's the beginning to my story and career, pursuing my passion as a 17 year old community college student and pursuing and developing my technical skillset.
